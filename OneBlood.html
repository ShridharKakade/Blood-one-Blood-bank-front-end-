DONATION PROCESS

The donation process from the time you arrive at center until the time you leave

REGISTRATION

You need to complete a very simple registration form which contatins all required contact information to enter in the donation process.

SCREENING

A drop of blood from your finger will be taken for a simple test to ensure that your blood levels are proper enough for donation.

DONATION

After passing screening test successfully you will be directed to a donor bed for donation. It will take only 6-10 minutes.

REFRESHMENT

you can also stay in the sitting room until you feel strong enough to leave the center. You will receive refreshments in the donation zone

Pre donation instructions

Get enough sleep on the night of the donation.

Eat a balanced meal two hours before donating.

You should not drink liquids with caffeine.

You should drink more fluids than usual.

Donation instructions

Go to the reception

The donor starts their journey by contacting the registry.

Take blood sample

Take a blood sample and test to determine whether or not to donate.

Doctor's permit

The doctor makes a decision about accepting a blood donation.

Food

Before donating it is necessary to eat a meal, do not donate blood on an empty stomach.

Blood donation

The donation is made in the donation room.

Donor's certificate

After donating, the donor's certificate will be issued.

Post donation instructions

You should drink more fluids than usual.

After donating, take a break and snack.

After 10-15 minutes you can leave the place.

Avoid heavy physical activity for 5 hours.
